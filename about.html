I am a passionate Software Engineer based in Etawah, holding a degree in Computer Science from BIET. Also known in the online dev community by my moniker 'ninja', I have dedicated a significant part of my life to the world of programming.

How it started

I was a 12-year-old who loved playing with Legos when I first discovered videos of Lego robots on YouTube. That simple curiosity led me down a rabbit hole. Slowly, I learned about the endless possibilities of computer programs and experienced the sheer enjoyment of challenging myself to make my logic work.

Ever since that initial exposure, programming has completely defined my life. It evolved from a fascinating hobby into a core passion and, ultimately, my career path.

My Philosophy: In addition to being passionate about programming, I am always eager to challenge myself to do things I've never accomplished before. I strive to learn and improve my skills every single day.

YouTube & Community

Beyond writing code, I love sharing my knowledge and journey with others. Over the years, I've built a community on YouTube focused mainly on programming and competitive coding content.
